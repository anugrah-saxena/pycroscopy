0. The What and Why?

A python package for image processing and scientific analysis of imaging modalities such as multi-frequency scanning probe microscopy, scanning tunneling spectroscopy, x-ray diffraction microscopy, and transmission electron microscopy. Classes implemented here are ported to a high performance computing platform at Oak Ridge National Laboratory (ORNL).

1. The Who?

This project begun largely as the effort of materials scientists and physicists at the Center for Nanophase Materials Sciences https://www.ornl.gov/facility/cnms to implement a python library for their own use and the benefit of CNMS users and hopefully the wider community of scientists that do imaging and advanced characterization of materials.

2. The Structure?

The package structure is quite simple, with 4 modules: 1. io 2. processing 3. analysis 4. viz
